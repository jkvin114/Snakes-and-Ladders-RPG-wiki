    Tusk Attack[Lv1 skill]

  • Cooltime: 2 turns
  • Range:3 (7 to players that have mark of ivory)
  • Damage a player by ivory, deals

    10+(0.3 x attack power+0.45*(armor resistance + magic resistance))

    magic damage
  • Deals 30 more damage if the target has a mark of ivory

  • Cooltime:5 turns
  • Range:15
  • Leaves mark of ivory to a player and gives

    curse effect

  • Cooltime:10 turns
  • Passive effect: attack and magic resistance increase based on missing health
  • Attack and magic resistance increases by 80~150 (based on current health)

    • Dinosaur [Warrior]

    Claw[Lv1 skill]

  • On/Off skill
  • When activated, damage all players within 4 sqares with claw, deals

    5 + (0.5 x attack power) attack damage


    (The damage is reduced if there are more than 2 players in range)
  • Health deacreases by 5 every turn when activated.
  • Can be deactivated by pressing skill button again

    • Regeneration[Lv2 skill]

  • Cooltime:5 turns
  • Passive effect: movement speed +1 when fall behind
  • Heals total 90 + (0.4 x attack power + 0.2 x missing health) for 3 turns.
  • Cannot throw dice during the heal, can halt on pressing skill button again.
  • Gains

    speed effect

    after use

    • Burning at the stake[Lv3 skill]

  • Cooltime:8 turns
  • Range:20
  • Damage a player with fire, deals

    40 + (0.7 x attack power + 50% of target`s missing health)

  • Cooltime resets if the target dies.

  • Cooltime: 3 turns
  • Range:20
  • Damage a player by gunfire, deals

    10 + (1.0 x attack power) attack damage

  • Cooltime:5 turns

  • Range:25

    speed effect

  • Range:15, Size:2

  • Cooltime:5 turns

    30 + (0.4 x magic power) magic damage

  • Cooltime:7 turns
  • Range:20
  • Try to devour a player, dealing
