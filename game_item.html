    Item levels

  • Lower level items can be upgraded to certain upper level items.
  • Price for upgrading is original item price discounted by lower level item prices.
  • The prices will be calculated and indicated automatically.
  • Example: The price for rare sword is 80$, but if you have 2 common swords (30$ each),
    you can upgrade them to rare sword by paying only 20$.
  • Only level 1 items can be bought in the first store.
  • Level 3 items are the highest level items.

    • Types of Abilities

    Attack Power(AD)
    • Increases damage of basic attack and attack skills
    Magic Power(AP)
    • Increases damage of magic skills
    Attack Resistance(AR)
    • Reduces damage from basic attack and attack skills
    Attack Penetration(arP)
    • Ignores certain amount of opponent`s attack resistance
    Magic Resistance(MR)
    • Reduces damage from magic skills
    Magic Penetration(MP)
    • Ignores certain amount of opponent`s magic resistance
    Damage Calculation
  • Actual attack damage = attack power * (100/(100 + opponent`s attack resistance - attack penetration))
  • Actual magic damage = magic power * (100/(100 + opponent`s magic resistance - magic penetration))
    • Health regeneration per turn
    • Regenerate certain amount of health every turn (applies up to 30)
    Absorb
    • Heals on attack proportional to the damage
    Basic Attack Range
    • Increases range of basic attack (applies up to 5 squares)
    Ult haste
    • Reduces the cooltime of level 3 skill (applies up to 3 turns)
    Movement Speed
    • Moves certain number more than dice number (applies up to 2)
    Adaptative Stat(adStat)
    • Added to higher stat between attack power and magic power
    Obstacle Resistance(obsR)
    • Reduces damage from obstacle
