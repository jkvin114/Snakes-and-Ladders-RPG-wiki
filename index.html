Welcome to Snakes-and-Ladders RPG wiki

Snakes-and-Ladders RPG is a tabletop role-playing game that combined traditional dice game(snakes-and-ladders) and RPG game.

How to win

The goal is simple: reach the end before other players

Various Features

Incorporates lots of features such as character skills, map obstacles, and 40 different items.

Comparison with traditional Snakes-and-Ladders game

  • In Snakes-and-Ladders RPG, there is kill ,which gives one more chance to throw dice, instead of Ladders, and there is death, which sends the player back to designated respawn(revival) square, instead of snakes.
  • The main difference is that these factors are determined by not only luck, but also the rpg-style battle between players.
