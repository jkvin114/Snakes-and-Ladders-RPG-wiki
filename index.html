Welcome to Snakes-and-Ladders RPG wiki

Snakes-and-Ladders RPG is a multiplayer role-playing game that combined traditional dice game(snakes-and-ladders) and RPG-style combat.

How to win

The goal is simple: reach the end before other players

Various Features

Offers various features that includes character-specific skills, obstacles placed on the map, and a wide selection of 40 unique items to choose from.

Comparison with traditional Snakes-and-Ladders

Snakes-and-Ladders RPG introduces a new twist to the classic game by replacing the traditional Ladders with a 'kill' feature, which grants an extra roll of the dice and money reward, and Snakes with a 'death' feature, which sends the player back to a designated respawn square. The gameplay in this version also emphasizes the role of strategy and battles with obstacles in addition to luck in determining the players' progress on the game board
