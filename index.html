How to win

The goal is simple: reach the end before other players

  • In Snakes-and-Ladders RPG, there is kill ,which allows to throw dice one more time, instead of Ladders,
    and there is death, which sends the player back to designated respawn(revival) square, instead of snakes.
  • The main difference is that these factors are determined by not only luck, but players` strategy of managing their HP, skills, money and etc.
